FinXT Website — Image Sourcing Quick Guide
==========================================

OPEN THE SPREADSHEET
  IMAGE_INVENTORY.csv — open in Excel, Google Sheets, or Numbers.
  Filter by Priority (1 = do first) and Status (Replace vs Review vs Keep).

RECOMMENDED FOLDER STRUCTURE (inside public/images/)
  heroes/          — 6 page hero backgrounds
  services/        — 15 service card images
  sectors/         — 5 sector images
  case-studies/    — 4 case study images
  flags/           — country flags (optional reorganise)
  forms/           — functional images (Fitzpatrick scale)
  brand/           — logo SVGs

FREE STOCK SITES
  Unsplash     https://unsplash.com
  Pexels       https://pexels.com
  Pixabay      https://pixabay.com

PAID (consistent series / easier matching)
  Shutterstock https://shutterstock.com
  Adobe Stock  https://stock.adobe.com

COMPRESS BEFORE UPLOAD
  Squoosh      https://squoosh.app
  Target: heroes < 200KB, cards < 80KB, format WebP

STYLE CHECKLIST (every image)
  [ ] Works on dark navy background (no white box edges)
  [ ] Professional B2B — not cheesy stock smiles or neon sci-fi
  [ ] Topic matches the slot exactly
  [ ] Not already used elsewhere on the site (check Duplicates column)
  [ ] Desaturated slightly + subtle navy/gold grade in Lightroom/Canva

PRIORITY ORDER
  1. Replace Tier 1 Case Studies hero (Mainpicture.jpg on hero)
  2. Replace Tier 2 Programme Management service duplicates (hero-banner, livestudies_banner)
  3. Replace Tier 3 all 5 sector images
  4. Replace Tier 4 all 4 case study card images
  5. Review Tier 1 page heroes and Tier 2 AI service cards

HOW TO SWAP IMAGES IN CODE
  Page heroes:     pages/[page].js (img src) + data/seo.js (OG image)
  Services:        data/services.js (image field per service)
  Sectors:         data/sectors.js
  Case studies:    data/caseStudies.js
  Home hero:       components/HeroSection.js

HOMEPAGE MOSAIC (auto-updates)
  data/services.js → homepageFeaturedServices pulls from service images.
  components/WhatWeDeliverVisual.js uses the same — no separate files needed.

INSPIRATION SITES
  McKinsey.com, BCG.com, IQVIA.com, Kantar.com, Stripe.com

REAL PHOTOS (best ROI)
  Leeds office exterior/interior
  Research session (with participant consent)
  Team headshot or group photo
  One real photo beats ten stock images for About + Contact heroes

WHEN YOU HAVE NEW FILES
  Tell the dev assistant the filenames — it can update all paths in one pass.
